My Innovative Creations

Public Transit System (PTMS)

The Problem: Public transit users often lack real-time information on vehicle locations and passenger capacity, leading to uncertainty and inefficient travel planning. Operational teams struggle with outdated manual systems for tracking vehicles and managing routes, impacting efficiency and commuter satisfaction. My Role & Key Contributions: As a backend developer, I was responsible for designing and implementing the core logic for the real-time vehicle tracking system. This involved leveraging Django to build robust APIs to process and manage live GPS data from transit vehicles. I integrated an Open Map API to visualize vehicle movements and dynamically display routes. A key contribution was developing the system to accurately track and present the number of passengers on board, aiming to provide commuters with comprehensive, real-time insights directly on their mobile devices. Key Technologies & Methodologies Used: Python, Django, SQLite, Open Map API, Agile methodologies. Impact & Outcome: This system was developed to significantly improve urban mobility by providing real-time data accuracy, reducing commuter wait times, and enhancing the overall transit experience for passengers and operators. While its full real-world deployment was halted due to the lack of investment in necessary GPS hardware, the project successfully demonstrated a robust, scalable proof-of-concept for modernizing public transit. Lessons Learned: This project highlighted the critical importance of understanding and addressing not just technical requirements, but also external logistical and investment dependencies for full-scale project realization.

It successfully demonstrates robust API handling and dynamic content rendering, inspiring curiosity about space. Future Enhancements: Integrating interactive 3D models of celestial bodies or historical mission trajectories.

Event Planner

The Problem: Event planning and venue management often face disorganization, lack of real-time information, and difficulty in coordinating between venue owners and customers. This leads to inefficient bookings, unclear venue availability, and poor event experiences. My Role & Key Contributions: I developed a web-based application to streamline event planning and venue booking. My contributions include implementing a robust booking system for venue owners and customers, integrating a weather API to display real-time weather conditions for chosen event dates, and developing a feature to show current venue occupancy or "packed" status. Impact & Outcome: This platform significantly simplifies event organization and venue management, providing critical information to both venue owners and customers for more efficient planning and improved event execution.
